# Security Hall of Fame

Researchers who have responsibly disclosed security vulnerabilities to Tegridy Farms and helped the protocol stay safer.

This page is where we publicly thank you (with your permission). If you'd prefer to stay anonymous after your report is resolved, just say so in your disclosure email and we'll skip your entry here.

## How to get listed

- Report a valid vulnerability through the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
- Work with the team through fix, patch, redeploy, and mainnet verification.
- After a mutually-agreed disclosure window, if you want public credit, we add your name (or handle, or pseudonym) to the table below.
