## Gap closed in this pass

**Cart / Favorites `storageError` had no UI consumer.** The contexts exposed
`storageError === "quotaExceeded"` after a failed `localStorage.setItem`, but
nothing rendered a banner — the user still saw the cart silently roll back to
its prior state with no signal.

### Files modified

- `frontend/src/nakamigos/App.jsx`
  - `CollectionView` now destructures `storageError` from both `useCart()` and
    `useFavorites()` and OR's them into a `persistBannerError` flag.
  - New `<div role="alert">` rendered just above the wrong-network banner inside
    `<main>` — amber styling (`rgba(251,191,36,…)`) to distinguish from the
    red wrong-network banner. Copy: "Browser storage is full or unavailable.
    Your cart and favourites won't persist past this session."
  - Banner is purely conditional; no banner when storage is healthy.
